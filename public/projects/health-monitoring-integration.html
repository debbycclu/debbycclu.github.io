Health Monitoring App

Streamlining Data Streaming for Reliable Health Insights

Overview

As part of my capstone project during my Master's in Information Systems (MSIS) at NYU, I collaborated with NYU Rory Meyers College of Nursing to enhance AppEase, a health monitoring app designed to support caregivers and children with ADHD and ASD. The app integrates medical research with advanced technology, leveraging the Apple Watch, iPhone, and a robust backend infrastructure to provide actionable insights into children's activities and stress alleviation. Our goal was ensuring the app working before real pilot test with patients, so we tested data streaming to ensure seamless communication between the Apple Watch, iPhone, and the database, enabling reliable health monitoring.
