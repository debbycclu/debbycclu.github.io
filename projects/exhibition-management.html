Exhibition Management Projects

Bridging Creativity and Operational Excellence

Overview

As a Planning Specialist at udnFunLife, one of Taiwan's largest media groups, I curated and managed high-profile exhibitions featuring Japanese animation and cultural themes. My responsibilities spanned end-to-end project management, from strategic planning to execution, with a strong focus on localization, stakeholder collaboration, and delivering exceptional visitor experiences.

1. Detective Conan: Scientific Investigations Exhibition (2019)

Role: Team Lead
Key Achievement: No. 1 exhibition in Taiwan (2019), $1M+ net profits

Responsibilities and Impact:

  • Strategic Localization: Adapted from Osaka format while maintaining Shogakukan Inc. guidelines
  • End-to-End Project Management: Oversaw manufacturing and operational workflows
  • Interactive Experience Design: Managed visitor engagement through detective manuals
  • Cross-Functional Leadership: Led team to record-breaking summer attendance

2. Studio Ghibli Layout Designs Exhibition (2018–2019)

Role: Ticket Operations Lead
Key Achievement: Managed 6,000+ daily visitors at peak

Responsibilities and Impact:

  • Ticketing Operations: Coordinated multi-channel sales and custom ticket designs
  • Venue Management: Directed 40+ staff members
  • Financial Oversight: Managed POS systems and cash handling
  • Live Event Coordination: Liaison for marketing initiatives

3. Fukui Prefectural Dinosaur Exhibition (2019–2020)

Role: Marketing Manager
Key Achievement: Exceeded marketing goals with $15K budget

Responsibilities and Impact:

  • Integrated Marketing Campaigns: Led design, digital promotions, and community engagement
  • Event Marketing: Organized multi-channel promotional activities
  • Press Conference Leadership: Directed pre-opening events
  • Budget Optimization: Delivered impactful campaigns within constraints
